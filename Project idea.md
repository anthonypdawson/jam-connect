## üîë Core Features

### 1. **Intent-Based Matching**

### 2. **Resonance Meter**
- Post-connection feedback tool to rate creative chemistry
- Helps refine future matches and build trust

- Emphasizes musical values, influences, and collaboration style

### 5. **Remote Integration**
- Optional links to JamKazam, Jamulus, or other jamming platforms
- Focus remains on connection, not latency optimization
